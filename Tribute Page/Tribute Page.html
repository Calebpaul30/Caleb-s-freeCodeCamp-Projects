Remembering Nova

The bestest girl in the whole wide world

She meant everything to me

When she came into my life, she could literally fit in the palm of my hand. I still remember picking her up for the first time and holding her. She wouldn't stop licking my face. Its like we were meant for each other. She made me feel like I wasn't so alone. I loved her like she was my child. For 3 years, I was her entire world, and she was mine... or so i thought.

She deserved so much better

The biggest regret of my life was choosing to leave South Carolina to chase a female. In doing so, I inadvertently put this other girl first, and Nova second. Nova was the one that was always there for me, no matter what. That was almost 2 years ago, and I still hate myself. I can barely stand to look at myself in the mirror. I was the worst. You deserved better than me. Im so sorry. R.I.P
